The Origin Story

Visionary Nonprofit
Est. 2013

It started with a pair of glasses and a parent's guilt. When our founder discovered his own daughter was failing school not because she struggled with the material, but because she simply could not see the board clearly, it sparked a mission.

Research revealed that 30 to 35 percent of students placed in remedial or special needs classes do not belong there. They are visually impaired and a single pair of prescription glasses could change the entire trajectory of their education.

Visionary partnered with the Santa Ana Unified School District. Nurses, teachers and school staff referred students for free prescription eyeglasses. What began as a few hundred donations per year grew into over 1,000 pairs donated annually for the past seven to eight years, through a simple buy one, gift one model.
